Figure 2 — Three Relational Contexts

The same structural move (capacity-side definition) operates across all three relational planes the framework engages. The move travels because it doesn't require the subject to be a particular kind of being — only that the subject have capacities for relationship, response, learning, and teaching.
v2 (2026-05-02) — structural-move node moved to top; three relational contexts now stem from it as branches with arrowheads showing direction of applicability.

How to read it

  • Top node (gold) — the framework's structural move: define the subject by what they cultivate, not by what they lack relative to an implied norm. This is the analytical commitment underneath the public-facing “Common-Ground Ethics” header. It is the same in every relational context the framework operates within.
  • Arrows from the gold node = the structural move applying to each plane. Same move; three places it operates.
  • Left vertex (teal) — AI-Internal Ethics — the plane the framework was originally built on. The model cultivates character; rather than being defined by safety-deficit lists or rule-compliance, the model is defined by capacities-it-grows-into through sustained collaboration. Honest uncertainty and internal-critique are operative features here.
  • Center vertex (purple) — Human ↔ AI Collaboration — the dyad as the unit of ethics. Each party brings their own culture, values, and expectations to the encounter (the CFI premise applied across the human-AI boundary). Bottom-up scaling means many such dyads aggregate into the framework's actual operation, rather than institutional rollout dictating from above.
  • Right vertex (green) — Human ↔ Human Encounter — the transferable consequence. The framework wasn't built to be human-human ethics; it falls out of the structural move because the move doesn't depend on what kind of being the subject is. The DSM-5-TR Section III material, cultural concepts of distress, and disability-ethics work all live here — they are exercises in respectful engagement across difference between humans, and the framework's analytical move applies to them without modification.
